# Academic Policies

## Grading Scale
- A: 90-100% (4.0)
- B: 80-89% (3.0)
- C: 70-79% (2.0)
- D: 60-69% (1.0)
- F: Below 60% (0.0)

## Academic Standing
- Good Standing: GPA >= 2.0
- Academic Probation: GPA 1.5-1.99
- Academic Suspension: GPA < 1.5 for two consecutive semesters

## Withdrawal Policy
- Students may withdraw from courses through Week 8 with a "W" grade
- After Week 8, withdrawals require Dean approval
- Full semester withdrawal requires advisor consultation

## Financial Aid
- Minimum GPA requirement: 2.0
- Completion rate requirement: 67% of attempted credits
- Maximum timeframe: 150% of published program length
- SAP Appeals must be submitted within 30 days of notification

## Academic Integrity
- Plagiarism, cheating, and unauthorized collaboration are prohibited
- First offense: F on assignment
- Second offense: F in course
- Third offense: Academic suspension

## Accommodations
- Students with disabilities should register with Accessibility Services
- Documentation required within 14 days of request
- Accommodations are retroactive only in exceptional circumstances

## Transfer Credits
- Maximum 60 transfer credits accepted
- Credits must be from regionally accredited institutions
- Minimum grade of C required for transfer
- Evaluation completed within 30 days of transcript receipt
