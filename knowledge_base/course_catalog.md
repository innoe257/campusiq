# Course Catalog 2024-2025

## Computer Science

### CS101: Introduction to Programming
- Credits: 3
- Prerequisites: None
- Description: Fundamental programming concepts using Python. Variables, control structures, functions, and basic data structures.
- Difficulty: 2/5

### CS201: Data Structures and Algorithms
- Credits: 3
- Prerequisites: CS101
- Description: Arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, graphs, and fundamental algorithms.
- Difficulty: 4/5

### CS301: Machine Learning
- Credits: 3
- Prerequisites: CS201, MATH201
- Description: Supervised and unsupervised learning, neural networks, model evaluation.
- Difficulty: 5/5

### CS401: Capstone Project
- Credits: 4
- Prerequisites: Senior standing, CS301
- Description: Team-based software engineering project with industry mentorship.
- Difficulty: 4/5

## Business

### BUS101: Introduction to Business
- Credits: 3
- Prerequisites: None
- Description: Overview of business functions, management, marketing, finance, and operations.
- Difficulty: 2/5

### BUS201: Financial Accounting
- Credits: 3
- Prerequisites: None
- Description: Principles of financial accounting, balance sheets, income statements, cash flow.
- Difficulty: 3/5

### BUS301: Marketing Strategy
- Credits: 3
- Prerequisites: BUS101
- Description: Market analysis, consumer behavior, brand management, digital marketing.
- Difficulty: 3/5

## Psychology

### PSYCH101: Introduction to Psychology
- Credits: 3
- Prerequisites: None
- Description: Scientific study of behavior and mental processes.
- Difficulty: 2/5

### PSYCH201: Research Methods
- Credits: 3
- Prerequisites: PSYCH101
- Description: Experimental design, statistical analysis, research ethics.
- Difficulty: 3/5

## Engineering

### ENG101: Engineering Fundamentals
- Credits: 4
- Prerequisites: None
- Description: Problem-solving, engineering design process, technical communication.
- Difficulty: 3/5

### ENG201: Statics and Dynamics
- Credits: 4
- Prerequisites: MATH201
- Description: Force systems, equilibrium, kinematics, kinetics of particles and rigid bodies.
- Difficulty: 5/5
