# Program Requirements

## Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
- Total Credits Required: 120
- Minimum GPA: 2.0
- Core Courses: CS101, CS201, CS301, CS401
- Math Requirements: MATH101, MATH201, MATH301
- Science Requirements: PHYS101, PHYS201

## Bachelor of Business Administration
- Total Credits Required: 120
- Minimum GPA: 2.0
- Core Courses: BUS101, BUS201, BUS301, BUS401
- Math Requirements: MATH101, STAT201
- Electives: 24 credits

## Bachelor of Arts in Psychology
- Total Credits Required: 120
- Minimum GPA: 2.0
- Core Courses: PSYCH101, PSYCH201, PSYCH301, PSYCH401
- Science Requirements: BIO101, STAT201
- Clinical Practicum: 6 credits

## Bachelor of Science in Engineering
- Total Credits Required: 128
- Minimum GPA: 2.5
- Core Courses: ENG101, ENG201, ENG301, ENG401
- Math Requirements: MATH101, MATH201, MATH301, MATH401
- Design Projects: 8 credits
